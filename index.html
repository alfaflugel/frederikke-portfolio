Something about me

My name is Frederikke, I’m 28 years old, and I am studying Multimedia design at KEA - Copenhagen school of design and technology. Currently, I am in my 3rd semester where I have chosen the elective Digital Design. I’m a very creative person and have a background in the beauty industry as a makeup artist and love to express myself creatively with digital tools.

I have a passion for digital design and dream of one day wotking with graphic design, prototyping, UI design and development of web solutions. I hope to better my skills in these areas in my internship. Furthermore, I look forward to applying the skills I have gained from my education in a professional context.

I am good at taking initiative and working independently, and I also function well when collaborating with others. I am excited to learn a lot and to demonstrate what I am capable of.
